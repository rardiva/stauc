About the Staunch Club.

The Staunch Club serves as a center for male influence and development at the Papua New Guinea University of Technology. Its mission is to break barriers and foster a sense of nationalism among young Papua New Guinean professionals. The club strives to unite young men regardless of religious beliefs, ethnic background, or social status, creating a space where they can grow intellectually, cultivate discipline, adopt healthy lifestyles, contribute meaningfully to society, and emerge as respectable citizens.

In 2023, Gerard Ivalaoa and Leeroy Sakarias envisioned a platform dedicated to empowering and inspiring male students at the university to become integral leaders. Gerard, having spent three years working with non-governmental organizations to uplift young Papua New Guineans, sought to bring his experience to a broader academic community. Leeroy, a seasoned educator in the Papua New Guinea Education Department specializing in Advanced Mathematics and Physics for Grade 11 and 12 students, aspired to establish a space where students could refine their intellectual prowess and leadership qualities.

After extensive discussions and planning, the two founders decided to create a foundational document to guide the development of the club. Drawing on Leeroy’s insights, Gerard took the initiative to draft the framework that would shape the club’s mission and operations. The Staunch Club was founded as a male center of influence with a clear vision: “To cultivate men with a higher sense of purpose and a moral obligation to serve God and country.” Its mission is “To develop young men into highly intelligent, influential, and innovative leaders.”

Fueled by passion, the co-founders sought to establish a space where men uplift one another—a club where students critically reflect on their purpose at university and how their journey contributes to the nation of Papua New Guinea. Their goal was to inspire male students at the Papua New Guinea University of Technology to embody strong moral values, unwavering ethical principles, integrity in leadership, and imaginative thinking.

Symbolism in the Staunch Club Logo

    The club’s emblem carries deep meaning:
  • The clenched fist symbolizes confidence. Men should stand firm in their decisions, carry themselves with assurance, and interact with others with conviction.
  • The suit and tie represent discipline. Intellectual discipline must reflect in a man’s character, demonstrated through professionalism and the way he presents himself.
  • The shield signifies leadership. Men are called to lead—not follow. They must first guide themselves before taking on responsibilities in their families, communities, and country.


    • The Role of Fashion

    Fashion plays a significant role in the Staunch Club. Members are expected to uphold high standards of ethics and professionalism by dressing in suits and ties for club programs and events. University male students should take pride in their attire and attend lectures dressed as young professionals, reinforcing confidence and self-respect.

    Building Public Speaking Excellence

    The Staunch Club serves as a platform for young men to refine their public speaking skills—an essential asset for project presentations, job interviews, and collaboration in professional industries. Members are encouraged to be articulate, expressive, and effective communicators.

    Promoting Intellectual Development

    A cornerstone of the club’s activities is reading, which fosters intellectual growth and critical thinking. Books will be provided to members, who will then submit summaries and reflections to be shared on the club’s social media platforms. Engaging with literature is an essential part of every Staunch Club member’s journey toward knowledge and self-improvement.

    Health and Well-being

    Health is paramount in the Staunch Club, and members will participate in group workouts to promote physical fitness and discipline. Activities such as morning jogs, hiking, and swimming will help members maintain strong physiques and overall well-being. In addition to fitness, movie nights and social events will be held to support members’ mental, social, and spiritual welfare, ensuring holistic personal development.

    Advocacy for Change

    Staunch Club members are youth ambassadors for change, actively involved in community advocacy. One of the club’s major awareness programs focuses on tuberculosis (TB) prevention. Members will undergo TB screenings to maintain their health and will engage in school and community outreach programs to raise awareness about the disease. TB is curable, and the club aims to combat stigma by encouraging testing and treatment in Papua New Guinea.

    Building Future Leaders

    The Staunch Club serves as a platform for leadership and personal growth, providing members with opportunities to develop communication skills, build confidence, and gain exposure to youth empowerment programs and trainings. Through these initiatives, the club shapes well-informed, visionary, and integral young leaders who will drive Papua New Guinea’s future.
