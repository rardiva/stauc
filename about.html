Who We Are

Launching of the Staunch Club.

The name, ‘Staunch Club’ is hereby defined as: staunch means to steadfast in principle, adherence and or loyalty; club means an association dedicated to common interests, goals and visions.

The name Staunch Club is hereby defined as a male center of influence in the Papua New Guinea University of Technology campus. The Staunch Club is an independent organization core founded by Gerard Ivalaoa and Leeroy Sakarias as a center of influence where men build men.

The Staunch Club guided by its own constitution and functions on its charter. The charter outlines the purpose and vision of the club. It is the wing that lifts the club to the future of the club.

The staunch club’s aim is to break barriers and create a sense of nationalism in young Papua New Guinean males. It aims to unite young men regardless of religious beliefs, ethnic background or social status with a vision to produce young Papua New Guineans who are highly intellectual, well discipline, develop healthy lifestyle, contribute meaningfully to society, and become a respectful citizen.
